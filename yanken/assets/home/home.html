About Us

During the Covid 19 pandemic, enterprices needed to transform their workspaces to a remote model, but for some businesses, this required IT capabilities than they possessed in house. Yanken Technology Outsourcing Services allowed businesses to hire already teams to prepare their organization to function remotely.

Yanken is a software development service partner for enterprises that need to outsource their web application and mobile app development as well as agile project management outsourcing, infrastructure, directing strategy, maintenance, running the service desk, and other IT related functions.
